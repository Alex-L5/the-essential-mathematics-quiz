The Essential Mathematics Quiz

Welcome to the quiz!

Click on Play to start, then answer the questions that appear. Select one answer out of 4 choices. The right answer generates 10 points, while there are no points for an incorrect answer. After you have completed the quiz, the High Score section shows the number of times a participant scored above 5 points. To check how you scored, you can enter your username and save it in the input box. You also have the option to start the quiz again. You can also click the buttons at the top left, i.e. Home, Start Game, High Scores and End Game to move through the quiz sections.
